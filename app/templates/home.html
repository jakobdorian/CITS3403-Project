Memory Flash

Welcome to Memory Flash!
The memory game to improve and test concentration.

How To Play

  • Click the play button and remember the coloured tiles in 1 second.
  • Click on all the tiles that were coloured in navy.
  • Be careful as you will lose points for incorrect tiles!
  • After finding all the correct tiles or 6 incorrect tiles, you will get a score for the round.
  • Play 2 more times to receive a final score.
  • Aim for the highest score possible and challenge your friends daily!
