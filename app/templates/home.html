Memory Flash

Welcome to Memory Flash!

The memory game to improve and test concentration.


In ancient times, the ability to memorise was a prized skill. According to Northwestern University psychology professor Paul Reber, our brains can store up to 2.5 petabytes of data. That’s the same as three million hours of TV shows!

Having a good memory has been proven to boost brain health, problem solving abilities, reading comprehension and focus. Memory strength is just like muscular strength, so the more you use it, the stronger you’ll get. Playing this game is a great way to strengthen your brain’s memory capacity.

It’s time to take control of your memory, start absorbing all the information that comes to you, and always have it at your fingertips!


How To Play

Click the play button and remember the coloured tiles
Click on all the tiles that were coloured in

Be careful as you will lose points for incorrect tiles!

10 points awarded for each correct tile (blue)!

3 points deducted for each wrong tile (red)!

After finding all 10 correct tiles or 6 incorrect tiles, you will get a score!

Remaining tiles will be revealed in green!

Play 2 more times to receive a final score!

The Final Score is the Average of the 3 Rounds!

Aim for 100 points and challenge your friends daily!
